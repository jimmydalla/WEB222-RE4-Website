About Resident Evil 4

Resident Evil 4 Remake is a 2023 survival horror game developed and published by Capcom. It is a remake of the 2005 game Resident Evil 4. Players control the US agent Leon S. Kennedy, who must save Ashley Graham, the daughter of the United States president, from the mysterious Los Illuminados cult. The remake retains the core gameplay of the original game, but with updated graphics, controls, and mechanics. The graphics are powered by the RE Engine, which was also used for Resident Evil 2 Remake and Resident Evil 3 Remake. The controls have been modernized to feel more fluid and responsive. And the mechanics have been tweaked to make the game more challenging and immersive. The remake also features a reimagined storyline that expands on the events of the original game. Some characters have been redesigned, and new story elements have been added. This makes the remake feel fresh and exciting, even for fans who are familiar with the original game. Resident Evil 4 Remake was released on March 23, 2023 for PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox Series X/S, and Microsoft Windows. It received critical acclaim, with praise for its updated visuals, gameplay, and story. It was also a commercial success, selling over 3 million copies in its first week of release.

Main Characters

Leon is a former Raccoon City Police officer who is now a federal agent working for the Division of Security Operations (DSO). He is a skilled and experienced agent who is determined to save Ashley Graham, the daughter of the United States president, from the mysterious Los Illuminados cult.

Ashley is the daughter of the United States president. A 20-year-old college student who is kidnapped by the Los Illuminados cult. She is initially portrayed as a damsel in distress, but she eventually proves to be resourceful and capable.

Luis is a Spanish biologist who works for the Los Illuminados cult. Initially a reluctant ally of Leon Kennedy, eventually a trusted friend and confidant of Leon.

Ada Wong is a mysterious spy who appears in the game to help Leon S. Kennedy. She is working for her own agenda, which is to obtain a sample of the dominant Plaga parasite.

Jack Krauser is a former major in the United States Army and a former partner of Leon S. Kennedy. He is also a traitor who has aligned himself with Albert Wesker and the Los Illuminados cult.
